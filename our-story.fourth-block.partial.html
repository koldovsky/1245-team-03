Our Story

2011

November

A small group of young like-minded artisans decided to handcraft a small collection of leather accessories, the goods which are much in demand in everyday life.

2012

February

In 2013, after two years of hard work and after receiving a grant from a well-established local enterprise, we moved our yet small workshop to the ex-farm.

2016

May

During three years we strove to grow our opportunities, equipment range, produce assortment, and what's most important, our dedicated team to achieve better results.

2017

July

We were recognized as the leading company manufacturing handcrafted leather accessories and well-known for our commitment to the highest quality and exceptional customer service.
